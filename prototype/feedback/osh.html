Hamburger

The hamburger menu lets you hide and show the off canvas menu so it’s only there when you need it. Hide the menu when you’re concentrating on filling out a tool or show it when you’re clicking about between various parts of the application.

Tip: You may switch this on screen help feature on or off at any time by clicking on the ' ? ' at the top right on the toolbar.
